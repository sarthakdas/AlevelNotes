Particle Interactions

When two particles interact, something must happen to let one particle know that the other one's there. That's the idea behind exchange particles.

Forces are caused by particle exchange, these exchange particles are called gauge bosons.

The reputation/attraction between two protons are caused by the exchange of virtual photons, which are the gauge bosons of the electromagnetic force. Gauge Bosons are virtual particles, and only exist for a short amount of time.

Interaction Diagrams

Rules for drawing interaction diagrams:

  • Incoming particle starts on the bottom of the diagram and moves upwards.
  • The baryons and leptons cant cross each other
  • Charges on both sides must stay the same
