Paper and Board Forming Processes

Die Cutting and Creasing

This is used to cut paper or card 'nets' which can be folded or assembled to form a 3d shape. They use a plywood substrate board into which steel cutting dies are inserted.

Bending

The stamped out net is placed onto a folding table

Laser cutting

This can engrave, cut, perforate and carve. These are perfect for small scale and small batch production. They are flexible on what they produce.
