Sheet and Film

Polymer based sheet and film are produced at various thicknesses and all have different purposes.

Foam Board

Two outer layers of card with a foam middle core, available in a range of thicknesses

Mood board backing, presentation boards, modelling

Fluted PP Sheet

Extruded sheets with integral 'flutes' or corrugations. Lightweight and easily bent along the flutes

Signs, Storage boxes, Portfolio cases

Translucent PP Sheet

Extremely tough polymer sheet. Can be scored before bending. Impossible to tear, water resistant

Packaging products, folders, boxes

Styrofoam

Dense, closed cell foam. Can be cut and sanded using standard workshop tools

Product modelling, formers for moulding and laminating

Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) Sheet

Tough, available in sheet form, transparent, flexible, good chemical resistance

Food wrapping, Bubble wrap, Carrier bags

Plastazote Foam

Closed cell polyethylene foam, tough, flexible, good impact resistance, impermeable to liquids

Protective packaging, swimming floats, gym and exercise mats

Cellulose Foam

Transparent, tough, bio-degradable

Packaging film, membranes, photographic film

Polylactide

Transparent, tough, bio-degradable

Bio-degradable packaging film
