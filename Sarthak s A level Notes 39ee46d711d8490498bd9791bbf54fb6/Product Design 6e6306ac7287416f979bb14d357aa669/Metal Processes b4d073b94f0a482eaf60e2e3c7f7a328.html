Metal Processes

Forming - Where materials are deformed to form the required shapes

Redistribution - Cut away material to get desired place

Wastage - Cut away material to get desired place

Forming Processes

Press Forming

Used to make body panels. Often paired with stamping. High cost and generally used for large scale manufacture.

Spinning

It is an alternative process to press forming, for shapes such as steel kettles (with radial symmetry). It means spinning a sheet of metal while being spun over a mould.

Cupping and Deep Drawing

Used to form tube like shapes.

  • The pressing blank is clamped over a deep drawing die
  • A press pushes the punch into the blank
